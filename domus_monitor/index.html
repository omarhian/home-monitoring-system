Product Area

Our Product

HiddenEye is a home monitoring system launched in 2019 by Domus ©. The company's debut product aims at helping homeowners keep an eye on their home at all times, giving them peace of mind.

The product features temperature controls, lighting controls, and ensuring the front doors to your home are locked. If you already have a Domus © home monitoring system and would like to get readings for your system, please scroll to the Product Reader section to log in.

Temperature Controls

Change, check, or view history of the temperature settings within your home.

Lighting Controls

Turn the lights on or off through the app or check to see if you left a light on.

Lock Doors Controls

Give yourself peace of mind knowing your front door is locked or when it is opened.

About Domus

Domus © is a home comfort studio and company that was created by Blessy, Ritesh, Omar, and Brienne. Domus © strives to be known within the industry as a brand that is family friendly, trustworthy, and reliable.

The word Domus © derives from the Latin word for “home”. For most people, their home is their tranquil place to feel safe, happy, and warm. It is one of the most basic human needs. The word home – or rather Domus ©– reflects and embodies the image and thoughts we’d like to be associated with the brand.
